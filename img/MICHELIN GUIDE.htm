MICHELIN RESTAURANTS SELECTION

Kyoku
Japanses . Hong Kong Island/港岛 . From HKD300.00

Opened in 2016,this sophisticated Japanese restaurant comes divided into two,with a sushi bar on one side and a teppanyaki station on the other.There's also a high table in the middle for those having a drink and a couple of private dining rooms.If you're at the sushi counter you have a choice of three omakase,while teppanyaki is a la carte.The wild tuna sashimi is a memorable dish,and good use is made of the Japanese Wagyu
